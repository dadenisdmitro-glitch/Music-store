About UKMX Music Store

Located in downtown Reykjavík, UKMX Music Store is your premier destination for vinyl records, CDs, and exclusive music merchandise. We specialize in rock, pop, rap, trap, and house music, offering carefully curated collections for music enthusiasts of all kinds.

Since opening in 2016, UKMX has hosted local in-store performances, record-listening events, and community vinyl swaps. Our staff are passionate collectors who help source rare pressings and new releases from Icelandic and international artists.

What we offer:

  • Curated vinyl and CD selections across multiple genres
  • Official merch — t-shirts, tote bags, and limited-run items
  • In-store listening booths and weekly DJ nights
  • Record cleaning and basic pressing advice

We aim to be a cultural hub for Reykjavík's music lovers — whether you're hunting a rare pressing or just looking for your next favorite album, our collection and friendly staff have you covered.
